Nicolay Rusnachenko

A research scientist (PhD student) at BMSTU supervised by Natalia Loukachevitch at MSU, where work on Sentiment Attitudes Extraction task. The study is partially supported by RFBR. Meanwhile the job is devoted to development on .NET platform. The latter allows to consider the research as a toolkit development for Sentiment Attitudes Extraction.
